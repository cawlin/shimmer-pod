Rise & Fall is a two-player campaign game about adventure, consequence, and the living world that pushes back against those who dare to cross it. Inspired by *The Neverending Story*, *Tales of Earthsea*, and *Labyrinth*, the game captures the feeling of myth and journey — where the world itself is a character, where every path is a story, and where triumph and failure both leave their mark.

The game is not a race or a battle. It is two parallel adventures unfolding across the same mythic landscape, occasionally colliding in moments of high drama.

**Core Concept**

Two players each lead a Warband across a hex-based campaign map, starting at opposite ends. As they journey through the map, they alternate between two roles each round:

- **The Adventurer (Rise)** — controls their Warband, navigates the world, and seeks glory, treasure, and growth.
- **The Warden (Fall)** — embodies the world itself: its creatures, its villains, its hostile landscapes. The Warden runs the scenario, giving the world its voice.

The world is alive. The Warden is not a neutral referee — they are Fall incarnate, the force that opposes the Adventurer's rise. Every scenario is a contest between ascent and resistance.

1. **Warden** rolls for weather and conditions. **Adventurer** chooses a hex to move to.
2. **Warden** reads and sets up the scenario. **Adventurer** prepares their Warband, spending XP and equipping new items or abilities.
3. **Warden** runs the scenario. **Adventurer** plays through it.
4. Both players complete bookkeeping. Roles reverse.
